about

My digital journey began with the screech of a 56k modem, a sound that kicked off a decade-plus career of building, tinkering, and, on occasion, fixing what I accidentally broke.

I spend my days heroically preventing digital Armageddon. From Python to PHP, my toolkit is broad, and I'm fluent in a range of languages, including Visual Basic (yes, I said it, and it will always be Visual Basic!). I can even spot a Base64 encoded string on sight, a truly useless superpower.

While I've developed a genuine connection with most technologies, Ruby on Rails remains the friendly exception.
